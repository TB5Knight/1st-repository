    This is my digital art page, showcasing creations from the past year that reflect my identity. The designs symbolize various aspects of my life, including my school and favorite sport. Some pieces were made for special occasions like birthdays and holidays, while others focus on different professional sports teams in Florida. The first row describes a little bit about me. The second row of my three designs were for my family. The last row were designs I created for fun.

    This first design features the Orlando Magic, my favorite NBA team and the first team I saw play live.

    The second design is for the Tampa Bay Lightning, a hockey team my mom loved,which descibes my hometown.

    The final design represents my school, UCF, incorporating a space-themed element and palm trees, reflecting the motto "fear the frond."

    The first design was a birthday present for my nephew, featuring his favorite football team, the Miami Dolphins.

    The second design was for his older brother, showcasing the Tampa Bay Buccaneers as requested.

    The last design symbolizes family ties, representing my father who was a Florida Gator and Me who is currently a UCF Knight.

    The first image is suppose to be my nephews and I known as the "Big 3". They both wanted a basketball design with all three of us together.

    My second image is a custom design of Sonic the hedgehog and Shadow the hedgehog. Two hedgehogs who were compelety oposite from each other. One was representing the power of fire while the other represents ice.

    The last image is based off an anime show called My Hero Acadamia. It was a present for my nephews sixth birthday. The three characters in the image were the ones that the show wrap around on. The left is Katsuki Bakugo, right is Shoto Todoroki, and the middle, who is the protagonist of the series, is Izuku Midoriya.
